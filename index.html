2. Travel Site Form Search

What I hope to learn

Forms are a complex subject that require lots of detail. Forms help you collect information from users that visit your site. I hope to learn all the form controls HTML5 has to offer as well different types of attributes that go with them. I also hope to learn how to properly style the form and the inputs. The last thing but the most important is how to get the form to submit and process information.

How this relates to what I have/will learn in WDD

I believe that forms are an essential part to having a good website. Forms can be used from something as simple as a newsletter to something a bit more complex like a sign up form which allows users to sign up and create accounts. Maybe you have an eCommerce and you can use forms as a way for users to check out and pay. Forms are extremely helpfull and can be used in several situations that can only enhance the users experience as they visit your website.
